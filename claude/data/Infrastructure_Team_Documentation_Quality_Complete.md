## ‚úÖ REVISED RECOMMENDATIONS

### IMMEDIATE PRIORITY (Week 1-2):

#### 1. **Solution Summary Standards** (ALL TEAM)
- **Issue:** 40% of solutions are "Brief" or "Minimal", team avg 5.0/10
- **Action:** Establish 80-character minimum for non-trivial tickets
- **Template:** "Problem: [what was wrong] | Action: [what was done] | Outcome: [result]"
- **Focus:** Luke Mason (10% empty), Daniel & Abdallah (minimal summaries)

#### 2. **Daniel Dignadice & Abdallah Ziadeh - Manual Comment Training**
- **Issue:** Only 4.3% and 16.4% manual comments respectively
- **Action:** Require worknotes for all troubleshooting steps
- **Mentor:** Pair with Tash (80.5% manual) for 2 weeks
- **Target:** 40% manual comment ratio minimum

#### 3. **Luke Mason - Closure Discipline**
- **Issue:** Good at comments (7.6/10) but terrible at solutions (3.8/10, 10% empty)
- **Action:** Cannot close ticket without 80+ char solution summary
- **Training:** Review his worknotes and convert to solution summary
- **Target:** Zero empty solutions, 5.5/10 solution quality minimum

### SHORT-TERM (Month 1-2):

#### 4. **Tash Dadimuni & Zankrut - Solution Summary Coaching**
- **Issue:** Excellent comments (10/10, 9.2/10) but average solutions (5.1/10, 5.2/10)
- **Action:** Convert worknote detail to solution summaries
- **Training:** Review 10 tickets - show how to write closure summary from worknotes
- **Target:** Leverage their comment quality for better solutions

#### 5. **Documentation Quality Dashboard**
- **Metrics:**
  - Solution quality score (target: 7/10 average)
  - Manual comment ratio (target: 40% minimum)
  - Empty solution rate (target: 0%)
- **Review:** Weekly manager review per team member
- **Recognition:** Monthly "Best Documenter" (both channels)

### LONG-TERM (Month 3+):

#### 6. **Two-Channel Documentation Standards**
- **During Work:** Worknotes for troubleshooting steps (40% manual minimum)
- **At Closure:** Comprehensive solution summary (80+ chars, problem-action-outcome)
- **Performance KPI:** Combined documentation quality score
- **Target:** 7/10 solution quality + 40% manual comments = "Good" rating

#### 7. **Role Model Mentoring Program**
- **Solution Summaries:** Llewellyn Booth (6.1/10) mentors Luke, Daniel, Abdallah
- **Comment Engagement:** Tash Dadimuni (80.5% manual) mentors Daniel, Abdallah
- **Overall Excellence:** Lance Letran (10/10 comments + good solutions) leads training
