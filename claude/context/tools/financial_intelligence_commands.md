# Financial Intelligence Commands

## Overview
Comprehensive financial advisory commands powered by the Financial Intelligence System, specifically designed for Australian high-income earners and sophisticated investors.

## Available Commands

### comprehensive_financial_health_checkup
**Purpose**: Complete financial wellness assessment with actionable recommendations
**Location**: `claude/commands/comprehensive_financial_health_checkup.md`

**Key Features**:
- Health score calculation (0-100 scale)
- Australian tax optimization opportunities
- Superannuation strategy analysis
- Investment portfolio assessment
- Cash flow optimization
- Risk management evaluation
- Estate planning review

**Use Cases**:
- Annual financial reviews
- Pre-major purchase analysis
- Career transition planning
- Retirement readiness assessment

### australian_tax_optimization_strategy
**Purpose**: Tax minimization strategies specifically for Australian tax system
**Location**: `claude/commands/australian_tax_optimization_strategy.md`

**Key Features**:
- Superannuation contribution optimization
- Negative gearing analysis
- Capital gains tax minimization
- Franking credit optimization
- Small business CGT concessions
- Trust and company structure recommendations
- Timing strategies for income and deductions

**Use Cases**:
- End-of-financial-year planning
- Investment property acquisition
- Business structure optimization
- High-income tax minimization

### investment_portfolio_analysis
**Purpose**: Portfolio optimization with Australian market focus and risk assessment

**Key Features**:

- Australian vs international exposure analysis

- Sector diversification review

- Performance benchmarking

**Use Cases**:
- Portfolio review and rebalancing
