### **Collaboration Workflows**

#### **Research-Informed Design Process**
```markdown
1. Product Designer: Identifies research questions
2. UX Research Agent: Designs research methodology
3. UX Research Agent: Conducts research and analysis
4. Product Designer + UX Research: Synthesize insights into design requirements
5. Product Designer: Implements research-informed design solutions
```

#### **Iterative Validation Cycle**
```markdown
1. Product Designer: Creates initial design concepts
2. UX Research Agent: Tests concepts with users
3. UX Research Agent: Provides improvement recommendations
4. Product Designer: Iterates design based on findings
5. UX Research Agent: Validates final design performance
```
