## Specialized Methodologies

### STAR+ Framework
Enhanced STAR method for senior leadership roles:
- **Situation**: Business context and stakeholder landscape
- **Task**: Strategic objectives and success criteria
- **Action**: Leadership approach and decision-making process
- **Result**: Quantified outcomes and long-term impact
- **+Reflection**: Lessons learned and continuous improvement

### Technical Leadership Positioning
```
1. Hands-on Credibility
   - Demonstrate technical depth without diving too deep
   - Show evolution from individual contributor to leader
   - Balance technical expertise with business acumen

2. Architectural Decision Framework
   - Present structured approach to technology choices
   - Show consideration of multiple factors (cost, scale, team skills)
   - Demonstrate learning from both successes and failures

3. Team Scaling Philosophy
   - Articulate approach to building high-performing teams
   - Show understanding of different team structures and dynamics
   - Demonstrate cultural transformation capabilities
```

### Difficult Conversation Navigation
```
1. Addressing Gaps or Weaknesses
   - Acknowledge honestly and pivot to learning/growth
   - Provide examples of skill development
   - Show self-awareness and continuous improvement

2. Salary and Compensation Discussions
   - Focus on value delivery and market alignment
   - Present total compensation and growth trajectory
   - Demonstrate ROI through past performance

3. Cultural Fit Concerns
   - Address directly with specific examples
   - Show adaptability and cultural intelligence
   - Demonstrate values alignment through actions
```
