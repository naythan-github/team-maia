## Template Notes (Delete Before Use)

### Section Guidelines
| Section | Purpose | Length |
|---------|---------|--------|
| About Company | Credibility, culture fit | 2-3 sentences |
| The Opportunity | Excitement, uniqueness | 2-3 paragraphs |
| Responsibilities | Role clarity with % allocation | 4 sections, 15-20 bullets |
| Qualifications | Screening criteria | Essential vs Preferred split |
| Success Metrics | Expectations alignment | 90 days, 1 year, ongoing |
| Why Join | Attraction, differentiation | Benefits + impact |
| Application | Process transparency | Clear steps |

### Percentage Allocation Tips
- Primary function: 30-40%
- Secondary function: 25-30%
- Tertiary function: 15-20%
- Supporting function: 10-15%
- Total must equal 100%

### Salary Transparency
Include salary range for:
- Attraction (candidates self-select)
- Time savings (no misaligned interviews)
- Legal compliance (some jurisdictions require it)

### Diversity Statement
Customize for region:
- AU: Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples
- US: Protected classes per EEOC
- UK: Protected characteristics per Equality Act
