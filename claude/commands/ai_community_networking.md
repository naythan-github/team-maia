### Target Community Segments

#### **1. Enterprise AI Leaders (40% of networking effort)**
**Profile**: CTOs, AI Directors, Chief Data Officers at large enterprises
**Value Proposition**: Business-AI bridge expertise, practical implementation experience
**Engagement Strategy**: Share enterprise AI transformation insights, offer consultation perspective
**Target Companies**: 
- Australian banks (CBA, ANZ, Westpac, NAB)
- Mining companies (BHP, Rio Tinto, Fortescue)
- Government departments (Digital Transformation Agency, Services Australia)
- Consulting firms (PwC, Deloitte, McKinsey AI practices)

#### **2. AI Startup Ecosystem (25% of networking effort)**
**Profile**: AI startup founders, technical leaders, product managers
**Value Proposition**: Enterprise sales expertise, business model validation, market insights
**Engagement Strategy**: Share startup-enterprise bridge insights, advisory opportunities
**Target Communities**:
- Australian AI startup founders (Sydney, Melbourne startup ecosystems)
- Y Combinator AI companies (global reach)
- AI accelerator alumni networks
- Venture capital AI portfolio companies

#### **3. AI Researchers & Practitioners (20% of networking effort)**
**Profile**: ML researchers, AI engineers, data scientists
**Value Proposition**: Real-world implementation insights, production system experience
**Engagement Strategy**: Bridge research-practice gap, share Maia system technical learnings
**Target Communities**:
- University AI research groups (UTS, UNSW, University of Melbourne)
- AI research labs (CSIRO Data61, Gradient Institute)
- Open source AI communities (Hugging Face, GitHub AI projects)
- AI conference speaker networks

#### **4. AI Conference & Community Leaders (15% of networking effort)**
**Profile**: Conference organizers, meetup leaders, AI community builders
**Value Proposition**: Speaking expertise, business perspective on AI trends
**Engagement Strategy**: Offer speaking, contribute to community building, share unique insights
**Target Events**:
- AI conferences (PyConAU AI track, AI Summit Australia, NDC AI)
- Local meetups (Sydney AI, Melbourne AI, Perth Tech meetups)
- Global conferences (NeurIPS business track, AI Summit series)
- Industry-specific AI events (FinTech AI, Mining AI, GovTech AI)
