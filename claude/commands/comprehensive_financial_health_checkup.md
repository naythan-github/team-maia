# Comprehensive Financial Health Checkup Command

## Purpose
Complete financial wellness assessment for high-income Australian executives, providing detailed analysis across all financial domains with actionable recommendations.

## Usage
"Run a complete financial health checkup" or "Assess my overall financial position" or "Financial wellness review"

## Inputs
- Current financial statements (assets, liabilities, income, expenses)
- Investment portfolio details (stocks, bonds, super, property)
- Insurance coverage information (life, income protection, TPD, health)
- Tax returns and current year income projections
- Debt information (mortgage, investment loans, credit cards)
- Financial goals and timeline objectives
- Risk tolerance questionnaire responses

## Outputs
- Executive Financial Health Dashboard
- Net worth analysis and trajectory
- Cash flow optimization recommendations
- Investment portfolio assessment and rebalancing suggestions
- Tax optimization strategies
- Insurance gap analysis
- Superannuation optimization plan
- Priority action plan with implementation timeline

## Implementation

### 1. Net Worth & Asset Analysis
```
- Calculate current net worth and asset allocation
- Analyze liquidity ratios and emergency fund adequacy
- Evaluate asset growth trends and performance
- Compare against age-appropriate benchmarks
- Identify asset allocation optimization opportunities
```

### 2. Cash Flow & Budget Optimization
```
- Analyze income vs expenses patterns
- Identify cash flow optimization opportunities
- Evaluate debt-to-income ratios
- Assess savings rate and investment capacity
- Recommend executive budgeting strategies
```

### 3. Investment Portfolio Assessment
```
- Portfolio diversification analysis
- Risk-adjusted return evaluation
- Asset allocation alignment with goals and age
- Fee analysis and cost optimization
- Tax efficiency of investment structure
- Rebalancing recommendations
```

### 4. Tax Efficiency Review
```
- Current tax position analysis
- Negative gearing opportunities assessment
- Capital gains tax optimization strategies
- Salary packaging and deduction opportunities
- Superannuation contribution optimization
- Trust structure benefits evaluation
```

### 5. Insurance Coverage Analysis
```
- Life insurance needs calculation
- Income protection adequacy assessment
- TPD coverage evaluation
- Health insurance optimization review
- Professional indemnity requirements
- Insurance cost vs benefit analysis
```

### 6. Superannuation Optimization
```
- Current super balance and performance review
- Contribution strategy optimization (concessional/non-concessional)
- SMSF vs industry fund analysis
- Investment option assessment within super
- Transition to retirement strategy development
```

### 7. Risk Management Assessment
```
- Investment risk tolerance vs capacity analysis
- Concentration risk identification
- Liquidity risk evaluation
- Career risk and income stability assessment
- Market risk exposure analysis
- Inflation protection adequacy
```

### 8. Goal Alignment & Progress Tracking
```
- Financial goal prioritization and feasibility
- Timeline and milestone establishment
- Progress tracking mechanism setup
- Scenario planning for goal achievement
- Adjustment strategies for changing circumstances
```

## Report Structure

### Executive Summary (2-3 pages)
- Overall financial health score (1-100)
- Key strengths and areas for improvement
- Top 5 priority recommendations
- Expected impact of recommendations
- Implementation timeline overview

### Detailed Analysis Sections
1. **Net Worth Dashboard**: Current position and 5-year projections
2. **Cash Flow Optimization**: Detailed expense analysis and recommendations
3. **Investment Strategy Review**: Portfolio analysis and rebalancing plan
4. **Tax Minimization Plan**: Annual tax optimization strategies
5. **Insurance Strategy**: Coverage gaps and optimization opportunities
6. **Superannuation Roadmap**: Contribution and investment optimization
7. **Risk Management Plan**: Comprehensive risk mitigation strategies
8. **Action Plan**: 12-month implementation roadmap with priorities

### Financial Health Scorecard
- Liquidity Score: Emergency fund and cash flow adequacy
- Investment Score: Portfolio diversification and performance
- Tax Efficiency Score: Tax optimization effectiveness
- Protection Score: Insurance coverage adequacy
- Growth Score: Net worth growth trajectory
- Planning Score: Goal clarity and progress tracking
