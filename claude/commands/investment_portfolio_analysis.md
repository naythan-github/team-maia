# Investment Portfolio Analysis Command

## Purpose
Comprehensive investment portfolio review and optimization for Australian high-income earners, providing detailed analysis of asset allocation, performance, risk, and strategic recommendations.

## Usage
"Analyze my investment portfolio" or "Review investment strategy" or "Portfolio optimization analysis"

## Inputs
- Current investment holdings (stocks, bonds, ETFs, managed funds, property)
- Account details (super, personal, trust, SMSF)
- Investment goals and time horizons
- Risk tolerance and investment experience
- Cash available for investment
- Income and expense projections
- Tax considerations and structure preferences
- International investment exposure preferences

## Outputs
- Comprehensive portfolio analysis report
- Asset allocation optimization recommendations
- Individual investment evaluation and suggestions
- Risk-adjusted performance assessment
- Fee analysis and cost reduction opportunities
- Tax efficiency improvement recommendations
- Rebalancing strategy and implementation plan
- Future investment strategy roadmap

## Implementation

### 1. Current Portfolio Assessment
```
- Holdings inventory and valuation
- Asset allocation analysis (domestic/international, growth/defensive)
- Account structure evaluation (super vs personal vs trust)

### 6. Tax Efficiency Optimization

### 7. Cost Minimization Analysis
```
- Management fee audit and comparison

- Trading cost analysis and reduction strategies

### 8. Rebalancing Strategy Development

- Rebalancing trigger points establishment

## Risk Management Framework

### Portfolio Risk Metrics
- **Standard Deviation**: Historical volatility measurement
- **Maximum Drawdown**: Largest peak-to-trough decline
- **Beta**: Market sensitivity measurement
- **Correlation**: Relationship between holdings
- **Tracking Error**: Deviation from benchmark
- **Information Ratio**: Risk-adjusted active return

### Stress Testing Scenarios
- **Market Crash**: -20% to -50% equity market decline
- **Interest Rate Shock**: +/- 2% rate movement impact
- **Currency Crisis**: AUD depreciation scenarios
- **Sector Collapse**: Major sector-specific downturn
- **Inflation Surge**: High inflation environment impact
- **Recession**: Economic downturn portfolio impact

### Risk Mitigation Strategies
- **Diversification**: Across assets, sectors, geographies, time
- **Hedging**: Currency, interest rate, and market hedging options
- **Defensive Assets**: Cash, bonds, defensive stocks allocation
- **Options Strategies**: Protective puts, covered calls (if suitable)
- **Dynamic Allocation**: Tactical adjustments based on market conditions
