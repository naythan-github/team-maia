## Australian Tax Law Compliance Framework

### Current Tax Rates & Thresholds (2024-25)
- Tax-free threshold: $18,200
- 19% tax bracket: $18,201 - $45,000
- 32.5% tax bracket: $45,001 - $120,000
- 37% tax bracket: $120,001 - $180,000
- 45% tax bracket: $180,001+
- Medicare Levy: 2% (plus surcharge if applicable)

### Superannuation Contribution Caps
- Concessional contributions cap: $30,000 (2024-25)
- Non-concessional contributions cap: $120,000 (2024-25)
- Carry-forward rules: Up to 5 years of unused concessional cap
- Total superannuation balance: $1.9 million general transfer balance cap

### Key Tax Dates & Deadlines
- July 1: New financial year, contribution resets
- June 30: Financial year end, final contribution cutoff
- October 31: Individual tax return lodgment (non-agent)
- May 15: Following year tax return (with agent)
